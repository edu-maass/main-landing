About

I believe finance is more than numbers—it’s a lever for transformation. Over the past decade, I’ve worked with SMEs, family businesses, and startups to help them structure capital, refine margins, and turn financial complexity into clarity.

As a Fractional CFO, I’ve seen how the right strategies—built on clear financial insight, precise valuation, and smart risk management—can do far more than improve the bottom line. They can unlock investment opportunities, accelerate growth, and create lasting value for both the business and the communities it touches.

My approach is hands-on and collaborative. I work closely with founders and decision-makers to align their financial structure with their vision, ensuring that every decision not only drives profitability and cash flow, but also builds a foundation for sustainable, meaningful growth.
